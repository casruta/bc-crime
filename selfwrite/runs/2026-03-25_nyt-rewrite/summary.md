## Key Learnings

1. **Scene-setting openings beat abstract data hooks.** The largest narrative-pull gain came from replacing "Ask British Columbians..." with "In Chilliwack, a city of 100,000..." Concrete places ground abstract statistics.

2. **Finding-first chart introductions are the highest-leverage single change.** Iteration 2 produced the largest delta (+0.45) by replacing mechanical "Figure X shows..." with finding-first intros ("The gap is widening"). This improved both Data Integration and Audience Calibration simultaneously.

3. **Compressing recommendations saves word count without losing substance.** Cutting "Why now" headers that repeated earlier findings removed ~100 words and improved scannability. Journalism integrates the so-what; it doesn't separate it into a recommendations section.

## Hypotheses That Worked

1. **Iteration 2** (data integration + audience): Finding-first chart intros + natural-language CSI alternates + sentence rhythm variation. Delta: +0.45. Improved two dimensions simultaneously.

2. **Iteration 4** (narrative pull): Scene-setting opening with Chilliwack. Delta: +0.25. Disproportionate impact relative to text changed (~3 paragraphs rewritten).

3. **Iteration 1** (narrative pull): Perception gap as mystery thread connecting sections. Delta: +0.25. "So why does nearly half the province believe the opposite?" creates forward momentum.

## What Didn't Work

No iterations reverted. All 4 produced gains. The 0-revert pattern suggests the rubric dimensions were well-chosen and low-hanging fruit was genuine at this score range (5-6). A longer run would likely encounter reverts as gains become harder.

## Convergence Behaviour

Score increased monotonically from 5.15 to 6.25. Gains diminished from +0.45 (iteration 2) to +0.15 (iteration 3) to +0.25 (iteration 4). With more time, the next targets would be Data Integration and Audience Calibration (both at 6), likely requiring more aggressive chart commentary and tighter sentence-level rhythm work. The Breakthrough Protocol did not trigger (no dimension at 7+ with stalled gains).
