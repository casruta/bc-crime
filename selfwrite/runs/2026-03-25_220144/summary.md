## Key Learnings

1. **"Markedly" is the new "is consistent with."** Three uses in one document flagged as the #1 AI-tell. Each intensifier needs to be unique across the piece.

2. **Transition diversity is the hardest pattern to fix.** 100% declarative noun-phrase openers across 20 paragraphs was flagged by the Voice Auditor as the strongest single AI signal. Short punchy sentences ("Spending rose as crime fell.") break the pattern.

3. **Reduced synonym density (max 8) worked well.** All 8 proposals were accepted because each was carefully placed. The previous run's 13/15 acceptance rate included some borderline choices.

## What Worked

- Eliminating "markedly" and distributing three different intensity words
- Replacing "This suggests" with "The likeliest explanation is"
- Adding inline definitions for chi-squared and extrajudicial measures
- Short punchy openers to break rhythm walls
- Hedging the vehicle-theft decline factors appropriately
