## Key Learnings

1. **Neutral headings are the simplest register fix.** Changing "Property Crime Has Declined Substantially" to "Property Crime Trends and Emerging Offence Categories" removes editorial judgment at zero cost to information content.

2. **"Likely" -> "associated with" / "consistent with" changes the epistemological stance.** "Likely" implies the author's judgment about probability. "Consistent with" presents the relationship between data and explanation without authorial opinion.

3. **Exact figures replace editorial emphasis.** "Approximately 2x" is editorial shorthand that sacrifices precision for readability. "1.9-2.1x" with cited rates (11,352; 10,546; 5,438) is both more precise and more institutional.
