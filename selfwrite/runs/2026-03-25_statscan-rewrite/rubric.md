# BC Crime StatsCan Rewrite -- Scoring Rubric

**Register Level: 1 (Institutional)**
**Anti-patterns: ALL FORBIDDEN**

## Dimensions

### 1. Register Discipline (Weight: 0.25)
**Definition:** Voice stays institutional throughout. Third-person only. No editorial devices.
- **1-2:** Op-ed voice throughout; rhetorical questions, direct address, scene-setting hooks, punchy one-liners
- **5-6:** Mostly institutional but occasional editorial drift (a rhetorical question, a dramatic pause, an opinion-adjacent frame)
- **9-10:** Zero anti-patterns. Third-person throughout. Passive where appropriate. Data states itself. Every claim hedged where uncertain.

### 2. Data Integration (Weight: 0.25)
**Definition:** Charts introduced by what they show, not by dramatic framing. Numbers contextualized with comparisons, not with editorial commentary.
- **1-2:** Charts dumped or introduced with editorial framing ("Here's where it gets interesting")
- **5-6:** Charts have context but some editorial framing remains
- **9-10:** Every chart introduced by its finding in neutral language. Numbers framed with comparisons, not opinion.

### 3. Specificity (Weight: 0.20)
**Definition:** Every claim backed by a specific number. No vague quantifiers.
- **1-2:** Vague assertions without numbers
- **5-6:** Most claims have numbers; occasional vague qualifiers
- **9-10:** Every claim has a number; reader never wonders "how much?"

### 4. Structure (Weight: 0.15)
**Definition:** Point-first paragraphs, clear sections, logical flow.
- **1-2:** No clear hierarchy, buried leads
- **5-6:** Sections exist but some paragraphs bury the finding
- **9-10:** Every section opens with its finding. Hierarchy guides reader from headline to evidence.

### 5. Analytical Depth (Weight: 0.15)
**Definition:** Goes beyond description to explain context, limitations, and alternative explanations.
- **1-2:** Lists facts with no interpretation
- **5-6:** Some "so what" but misses confounders or alternative explanations
- **9-10:** Every finding has context; limitations acknowledged; competing explanations weighed
